== Description ==

The Car Dealer Show theme comes packed with full-site editing, custom block patterns, and enhanced block editor features. 
This theme features a variety of sections (patterns) specifically designed for the car dealer business, enabling quick and efficient website creation.
Car Dealer Show is a multilingual and multicurrency theme for Car Dealer, Car Dealership, car listing, Automobile Dealer, Auto Dealer, Car Retailer, Automotive Retailer, Vehicle Dealer, Auto Vendor, Car Salesperson, Car Salesman, Vehicle Retailer, Automotive Merchantdealership to manage, list and sell your new or used cars, motorbikes, motorcycles and vehicles online. Usefull also for Trucks, Scooters, Side-By-Side, ATV, Utilitie ATV, Campers, RVs, Motorhome, Travel Trailer, Trailers, Lifts, Trator, Forklift & Lift Truck, Snowmobile, MotoSnow, Snow Motorcycle, Motorbike Motor Full, Agriculture Equipment and even Aircrafts, Jets Aircrafts, Helicopters, Turboprops, Charter, Airplanes, Aerotrader AirPlanes and much more,with our free MultiDealer plugin. Exclusive configurabile free plugin with Advanced search for cars.
This plugin enhances user experience with advanced customizable car search options, a price range slider, and versatile display templates, making it easier for customers to find exactly what they’re looking for.
Without the dedicated plugin, products must be manually inserted by editing HTML code, which is time-consuming and prone to errors. In contrast, the plugin offers a user-friendly interface connected to a database, allowing for easy car registration and management without any coding
Plugin also comes with 2 free templates (list view, gallery). Responsive, Social menu available and full color control (Eight pre-defined color skins with unlimited color customization options).   No coding required. With StartUp guide, guide online, support, faq page, demo. and 3 min demo video (www.CarDealerThemes.com)
For those who prefer a traditional theme without using the Block Editor, check out the Kar Dealer Theme.
